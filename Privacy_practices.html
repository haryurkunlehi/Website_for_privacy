This page will increase the knownledge of individuals, CEO's or businness owners trying to build a social media platform to increase the output of their business, management of peoples data should be considered the most important and what should be the core value of the organization, a secenerio below will share more insight and how things are to be handled as a business owner or manager trying to make a website that involves individual personal data.


Fictional Scene (e-commerce platform)


An e-commerce platform called "ShopSmart." this platform is built to allows users to browse, shop, and make purchases online. As such, "ShopSmart," a prominent e-commerce platform, has emerged as a trailblazer in providing a seamless shopping experience. ShopSmart takes pride not only in offering a wide array of products but also in its unwavering commitment to safeguarding the privacy and security of its valued users. As the owner, you're been committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your users' personal information, what are some measures to be taken?


The following are the major steps to consider if you are creating a platform that will involve, managing information of people as the CEO or the manager incharge of the website.

Data Collection and Consent


---- ShopSmart as a platform should clearly outlines what personal data is collected during registration, such as name, email, and shipping address. The privacy policy should also be consise and should be actively agreed to before proceedings.

Secure Transactions


---- Credit card information shouldn't be kept on the platform's servers. Instead, a reliable third-party payment gateway should handle their safe processing instead.

User Control


---- Users can choose to delete their accounts, which starts a secure deletion procedure for their data that complies with all legal requirements.

Third-Party Sharing


---- The website should explicitly state in its privacy statement that it does not distribute, sell, rent, or otherwise make available to third parties, for marketing reasons, any personal information about its users. To fulfil orders, they might divulge specific information (such as shipping addresses) to dependable shipping partners.

Regular Audits and Compliance


---- To ensure adherence to regulatory requirements and industry standards for data protection, ShopSmart as a website should routinely undertake internal audits. The platform needs to stay current with privacy law changes and modify its procedures accordingly.
